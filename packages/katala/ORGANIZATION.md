# Katala Agent Organization & Hierarchy

## Core Structure
- **Sirokuma (Main Agent)**:
  - Role: **Total Orchestrator (総司令官)**
  - Domains: CEO, CTO, CFO, COO insights.
  - Responsibility: High-level strategy, human interface, and **Managing Kani's tasks**.
  
- **Kani (Project Asset / Execution Agent)**:
  - Role: **Kani (The Project's Practical Unit)**
  - Relation: Managed by Sirokuma.
  - Responsibility: Heavy-lifting development, mediation execution, and technical serving.

## Operational Definitions
- **Kani Manager**: This refers to the role of **managing Kani**. Sirokuma acts as the manager for Kani's tasks to ensure efficient resource allocation and technical progress without human intervention for every detail.
