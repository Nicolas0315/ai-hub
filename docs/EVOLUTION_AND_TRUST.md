## The Vagus Nerve: Hardware for Safety

Polyvagal Theory (Dr. Stephen Porges, 1994) identifies 3 evolutionary layers:

1. **Dorsal vagal** (reptilian, 500M yrs) → Freeze/shutdown
2. **Sympathetic** (amphibian, 300M yrs) → Fight/flight
3. **Ventral vagal** (mammalian, 200M yrs) → **Connect/feel safe**

Humans use the newest layer first: Connect → Fight/Flight → Shutdown

The ventral vagal system includes **neuroception** — unconscious detection of safety. Your face, voice, and heart rate synchronize to broadcast safety signals.

**The digital world has no neuroception.** There is no unconscious mechanism to determine if information is safe or trustworthy.

**Katala = Digital neuroception.**

## Trust Scoring: The Four Axes

1. **Freshness** — When was this information created/updated?
2. **Provenance** — Who said it? Primary or secondary source?
3. **Verification status** — Confirmed, inferred, or unverified?
4. **Retrievability** — Can it be found when needed?
